# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.
- Take responsibility for your mistakes, apologize to those affected, and learn from the experience.
- Prioritize what is best for the community and the project.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — including the issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this project. It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@jolli.ai**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of anyone who reports an incident.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent consequences as determined by project leadership.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will use the following guidelines when determining consequences for violations:

### 1. Correction

**Impact:** Minor, isolated incidents such as use of inappropriate language or other unprofessional behavior.

**Consequence:** A private written explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate, with a request to correct it. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Impact:** A single more serious incident, or a pattern of minor violations.

**Consequence:** A formal warning with defined expectations for future behavior. Continued violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Impact:** A sustained pattern of violations or a single severe incident.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from all project interaction for a specified period.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Impact:** A demonstrated pattern of severe violations, sustained harassment, or aggression toward individuals or groups.

**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all project spaces.
